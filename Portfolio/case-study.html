Social Media Growth Campaign Instagram & TikTok

A digital marketing campaign focused on increasing reach, engagement, and audience growth through content strategy and performance analysis.

Project Overview

  • Client: Brand / Business Name
  • Role: Digital Marketing & Social Media Specialist
  • Platform: Instagram, TikTok
  • Duration: 3 Months
  • Objective: Increase reach, engagement, and brand awareness

Strategy

The strategy focused on consistent content publishing, audience-focused messaging, and performance-driven optimization.

  • Content calendar planning (feed, reels, short videos)
  • Audience research & hashtag optimization
  • Hook-based captions and CTA
  • Performance review every 7 days

Execution

Content was produced and optimized based on audience behavior and platform trends.

  • Designed social media posters & short-form videos
  • Scheduled posting with optimal timing
  • Iterated content based on reach & engagement data

Results & Performance

Content Insight

Growth in reach and engagement after content optimization.

Audience Engagement

Improved interaction rate from reels and carousel posts.

Google Search Console

Increased impressions and organic clicks after SEO optimization.

Key Takeaways

  • Consistent posting significantly improved reach
  • Short-form content generated higher engagement
